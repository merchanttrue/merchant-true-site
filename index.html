Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our consulting model.

Are you a merchant services provider?

expand_more

No. We are independent media and consulting. We don't process payments. We evaluate your setup and connect you with top processors at wholesale rates.

Are my uploaded documents safe?

expand_more

Yes. Your security is our priority. All uploaded statements are stored in **Supabase's encrypted enterprise-grade cloud storage**. We use strict access controls to ensure your sensitive financial data is only accessible to our authorized analysts during your audit.

How do you make money if you don't process?

expand_more

We earn consulting fees for audits or referral fees from our vetted partner network. Our goal is always the lowest possible rate for your business.

Can you help if I'm under contract?

expand_more

Yes. We specialize in identifying contract exit strategies or negotiating buyouts with new recommended partners.
